Avengers

Captain America



The leader of the Avengers, Steve Rogers was born in 1918 in Brooklyn, NYC. Suffering multiple health problems, including being severely underweight and weak, he more than made up for it with his courage and bravery. Volunteering for a secret military experiment, he undertook the super serum which transformed him physically into a super soldier. He enlisted and became one of America's greatest symbols, and soldiers. Leading the Howling Commandos, he fought throughout World War 2, stopped Hilter, beat the Nazi Red Skull.


In a tragic accident, Steve was thrown into the artic ocean. Because of his advanced abilities from the serum, he surived and was frozen. He was next awoken in modern day. Most all his friends passed away and he was alone in the world yet he found a new family, The Avengers. He now leads them alongside Iron Man safeguarding the world in the new era.


  • Alias: Captain America
  • Name: Steve Rogers
  • Strengths: Super-Strength, endurance, tactical genius, Good Man

Iron Man



Tony Stark is the CEO of Stark Enterprises, a former weapons company and he led a hedonist, and selfish, playboy style life until he was kidnapped by extremists. When he realized these terrorists were using the very weapons he created, he was shaken to his core. Building a metal suit to escape from them, he vowed to change his ways and do good. As an extension of this, he formed the Avengers with Steve Rogers and leads them together.


Yet he is not a man without demons. Despite the best of intentions, he is constantly narcissistic, impulsive, and reckless. Addicted to being Iron Man, he is self-destructive and has made many mistakes, least of which includes building the killer, self-aware robot Ultron. But despite this, he has a heart of gold and strives to do good.


  • Alias: Iron Man
  • Name: Tony Stark
  • Strengths: Super armor, creativity, intelligence, money, resourcefullness, ingenuity

Black Panther



T'Challa, the Black Panther, is one of the most unique Avengers. He is a superhero and the king of a country. Wakanda, the highly-isolationist, and technologically advanced African Nation is his home. He safeguards it using the power of the Black Panther mantle, that is passed from generation to generation.


He is respected not only for his combat skills, but his intelligence. A holder of multipler PhD's, he is one to fear in the field and out.


  • Alias: Black Panther
  • Name: T'Challa
  • Strengths: Intelligence, Expert Fighter, Resourcefulness, Monarch

Daredevil



Matt Murdock was blinded in an accident involving chemicals at the age of 5. He lost his eyes but in the process all his other senses were given extraordinary sensitivity and control. Because of this, he sees better than the normal person. Losing his father due to criminal corruption, he becomes a vigilante. He is a tortured soul who feels conflicted between his Catholic faith and his need to beat a vigilante and exact justice. In a perversion and expression of his faith, he dons a red devil costume to protect Hell's Kitchen.


  • Alias: Daredevil
  • Name: Matthew Murdock
  • Strengths: Martial Arts skills, Lawyer Skills, Endurance, Willpower, Enhanced Senses
